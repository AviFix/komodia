»Komodia » CurrentWare Partners With Komodia For URL Classification Data

Tel Aviv, Israel (See original on PRWEB) January 31, 2013

Komodia, provider of URL Classification Services, today has announced that CurrentWare, a global leader of Internet and Endpoint Security Solutions, has deployed Komodia’s URL Classification systems and services into their BrowseControl product, which is an employee monitoring solution.

CurrentWare’s CEO, Jay Lakhani, is very confident that the new Category Filtering feature will provide more granular Internet control, enterprise-wise. ”Our Internet filtering solution, BrowseControl, has successfully enhanced Internet security while boosting employee productivity. We are excited to introduce Komodia’s Category Filtering, which will provide an added benefit of blocking millions of objectionable web sites instantly with a few simple clicks.”

Barak Weichselbaum, CEO of Komodia, said this at the Press Conference: “Real time URL classification will be a great boost for CurrentWare products and offerings”.

CurrentWare, a division of Codework, Inc., is committed to delivering products with immediate benefits of increasing productivity, enhancing security and improving cost savings. The company reports that their products are intuitive to manage, and the user can better use their time to focus on solving real world computing challenges.

The CurrentWare product line-up include an enterprise level security solution, encompassing Internet filtering, web monitoring, endpoint security and power management functions. Their flagship solutions include: BrowseControl, BrowseReporter, AccessPatrol, and enPowerManager. BrowseControl is the industry’s most comprehensive Internet blocking and filtering solution.

BrowseReporter is a reliable and accurate solution for tracking and reporting on employees’ Internet browsing activity. AccessPatrol provides a proactive solution for securing company endpoints (USB, CD/DVD, BlueTooth, WiFi, iPhones, iPods, MP3’s, and more). EnPowerManager is a lightweight tool for effectively managing the energy usage of PC’s across an enterprise.

Recently, TopTenReviews presented the Bronze Award to CurrentWare for their Internet Security Solutions.
