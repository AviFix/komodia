Definitions

Software Product(s)

All computer programs and related documentation produced, owned, and/or licensed by Komodia, in source code form, as specified on Exhibit A. Software Product(s) include any Updates provided by Komodia hereunder.

Update

A release of the Software Product(s) that is developed and designated by Komodia as a change in the tenths digit in the version number [x.(x).x].

Software Product(s) Deliverables

Those deliverables specified on Exhibit A.

Composite Product(s)

Software products produced by Licensee for distribution hereunder that result from the integration and merger of Licensee products and/or code as specified on Exhibit A with Software Product(s) licensed and provided by Komodia hereunder.

Resellers

Companies or organizations that distribute software products to end-users.

Retail Channels

Resellers who purchase and resell product units and provide distribution and logistical resources for the ultimate purpose of stocking an inventory of tangible product media in retail stores for purchase by end-users.

Licensee Branding Features

Licensee’s proprietary trade names, service marks, trademarks, logos, and indicia of origin and other distinctive branding features as specified on Exhibit A.

Proprietary Rights

Any and all rights, whether registered or unregistered, in and with respect to patents, copyrights, confidential information, know-how, trade secrets, moral rights, contract or licensing rights, confidential and proprietary information protected under contract or otherwise under law, trade names, domain names, trade dress, logos, animated characters, trademarks, service marks, and other similar rights or interests in intellectual or industrial property

Derivative Works/Integration and Merger License And Restrictions

Subject to the terms and conditions hereof, including without limitation, Exhibit A attached, and only for term hereof, Komodia hereby grants to Licensee the right and license to create derivative works based on the Software Product(s) by integrating or merging the Software Product(s) to create Composite Product(s), using the Software Product(s) Deliverables to be provided by Komodia.

Bundling/Compilation License And Restrictions

Subject to the terms and conditions hereof, and only for the term hereof, Komodia hereby grants to Licensee the right and license to bundle and/or compile the Composite Product(s) only with product(s) provided by Licensee, provided that the Composite Product(s) remain a separate software offering.

Manufacturing License

Subject to the terms and conditions hereof, Komodia hereby grants to Licensee the limited right and license to reproduce, make, and have made finished Composite Product units suitable for marketing and distribution in accordance with the terms and conditions hereof.

Distribution License; Sublicenses; Restrictions

Subject to the terms and conditions hereof and only for the term hereof, Komodia hereby grants to Licensee the right and license to use, reproduce, publicly display, publish, and distribute the Composite Product(s) in object code form only, together with authorized derivative works based thereon, on a standalone basis, and as part of bundled products and/or compilations with other works only as expressly authorized above, and only through the authorized distribution channel(s) specified below. Licensee is authorized to grant sublicenses only in conformity with the Sublicense Terms provided on Exhibit C.

Authorized Distribution Channels

The distribution license granted above is granted both (i) for distribution to end-users located in the world through a single tier of distribution consisting only of sales personnel with face-to-face contact with end-users, and (ii) for distribution through the Retail Channels consisting of Resellers located in the world.

Branding Requirements; “Private Label” Rights

Licensee shall not incorporate any Komodia mark, logo, or other branding feature into the Composite Product(s), but Licensee shall incorporate OpenSSL designated copyright notice into Software Product(s) as specified by OpenSSL. Licensee is authorized to incorporate Licensee Branding Features into Composite Product(s). All distribution rights and licenses granted herein for Composite Product(s) are subject to the forgoing branding requirements.

Non-Exclusive Licenses

The licenses granted herein are non-exclusive. Komodia may compete with Licensee, and Komodia may grant licenses to third parties who may compete with Licensee, in connection with the Software Product(s).

Licensee shall, at its own expense, obtain and arrange for the maintenance in full force and effect of all governmental approvals, consents, licenses, authorizations, declarations, filings and registrations as may be necessary or advisable for the performance of the terms and conditions of this Agreement, including without limitation, fair trade approvals.

Reservation of Rights

All rights not expressly granted to Licensee herein are expressly reserved by Komodia.

Marketing And Support Obligations

Each party shall perform the marketing and support obligations in accordance with Exhibit B attached.

Royalties

Licensee shall pay to Komodia royalties in accordance with Exhibit A attached.

Title to the Proprietary Rights embodied in the Software Product(s) and in the Software Product(s) Deliverables shall remain in and be the sole and exclusive property of Komodia and/or its licensors.

Notwithstanding the forgoing, specifically regarding the Software Product(s), Licensee acknowledges Komodia's claim that the Software Product(s) source code embodies valuable trade secrets proprietary to Komodia. Accordingly, Licensee shall a high degree of care to protect the Software Product(s) source code from unauthorized access, disclosure, and use, including without limitation, the placement of any Proprietary Rights notice on the Software Product(s) that is reasonably requested by Komodia. Licensee shall not: (i) distribute, transfer, loan, rent, or provide access to the Software Product(s), except as provided herein; or (ii) remove or add any Proprietary Rights notice associated with the Software Product(s) without the express written permission of Komodia.

Injunctive Relief

The parties hereby agree that any breach of any provision of this Agreement regarding confidentiality or protection of Proprietary Rights would constitute irreparable harm, and that the aggrieved party shall be entitled to specific performance and/or injunctive relief in addition to other remedies at law or in equity.

Notices

Any notice or communication required or permitted to be given hereunder may be delivered by hand, deposited with an overnight courier, sent by email or facsimile (provided delivery is confirmed), or Israeli. Mail (registered or certified only), return receipt requested, in each case to the address set forth on the initial page hereof or at such other addresses as shall be designated in writing by either party to the other in accordance with this Section. Such notice will be deemed to be given when received.

Assignment

Licensee shall not assign this Agreement or any right or interest under this Agreement, nor delegate any work or obligation to be performed under this Agreement, without Komodia’s prior written consent. Any attempted assignment or delegation in contravention of this Section shall be void and ineffective.

Licensee shall have no authority to enter into agreements of any kind on behalf of Komodia and shall not have the power or authority to bind or obligate Komodia in any manner to any third party.

Notwithstanding anything to the contrary contained herein, if either party is unable to perform hereunder for a period of thirty (30) consecutive days, then the other party may terminate this Agreement immediately without liability by ten (10) days written notice to the other.

Location of Services

All services hereunder shall be performed at Komodia’s facilities or at the facilities of the Komodia Site(s), unless otherwise agreed in writing by Komodia.

Technical Support

If Licensee has questions about the Software Product(s), or believes that any Software Product(s) Deliverables do not conform to all of the requirements of this Agreement, Licensee may notify Komodia by telephone or through Komodia’s designated electronic mail support address, Komodia will use its best efforts to correct the error, within a commercially reasonable time. (average fix time for errors is 3 business days). The technical support services provided herein shall be provided without additional charge as long as the licensee in under a support service agreement.

Publicity

Komodia shall not use the name of Licensee in publicity releases, advertising, or similar activity without the prior written consent of Licensee which shall not be unreasonably withheld or delayed.

Deliverables

Promptly after the Effective Date, Komodia shall transmit to Licensee “gold master” copies of the Software Product(s) in a form and format that is mutually agreeable between the parties.

Updates

Komodia shall provide Licensee with reasonable prior notice of any Updates for the Software Product(s), and Komodia shall provide master copies of such Updates to Licensee without additional charge for the duration thirty (30) days, unless licensee in under a support service agreement which extends this duration.

Licensee’s Obligations

Licensee shall perform the following obligations at its cost and expense, unless otherwise indicated.

Technical Contact

Within five (5) days of the Effective Date, Licensee shall appoint and notify Komodia of its technical contact. Licensee’s technical contact shall coordinate all technical issues on behalf of Licensee which arise out of this Agreement, including without limitation, the obligations of Licensee specified in this Exhibit C

No Obligation to Market, Promote, And Publish
